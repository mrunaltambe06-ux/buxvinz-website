About Us

AG BUXVINZ & CO is a Mumbai Sutar Chawl based manufacturer of all kinds of packaging – jewellery boxes, gifting boxes, hampers, baskets and more. We focus on strong build quality, neat finishing and clean branding for jewellers & premium brands.
